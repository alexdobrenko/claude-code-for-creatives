FAQ

What is Claude Code?

It's an AI from Anthropic that runs in your terminal and can read and write files on your computer. Unlike ChatGPT, it can actually build things — not just talk about them.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. You need to be okay typing commands in a terminal window. But Claude writes all the code. You just describe what you want in plain English.

What if I can't make the live calls?

All calls are recorded. But the live calls are where the best stuff happens — people asking questions, troubleshooting together. Try to make at least 2-3.

Is this only for writers?

No. It's for anyone who makes things and wants to own their tools. Writers, designers, podcasters, freelancers, solopreneurs.
