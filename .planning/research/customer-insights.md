# Customer Insights from Call Analysis

**Source:** Granola call transcripts with Chris, Lawrence, Paul, Russ
**Analysis date:** 2026-01-19

---

## The Core Insight

**It's not about tools. It's about a mindset shift.**

People aren't coming because they want to "build apps." They're coming because they feel:
- Overwhelmed by AI possibilities
- Afraid of breaking things or doing it wrong
- Trapped by platforms they don't control
- Like they're missing out on something big

## The Real Transformation

**NOT:** Non-coder → Builds apps
**ACTUAL:** Afraid and overwhelmed → Empowered and curious

---

## What People Actually Want

1. **Permission** — to just ask Claude, to experiment, to break things
2. **Agency** — the feeling of "I can just do this now"
3. **Ownership** — files on their computer, not trapped in platforms
4. **A guide** — someone who makes the scary thing feel doable
5. **Possibility** — seeing what's possible, not being sold productivity

---

## Patterns in What Lands

- The Chief of Staff / values integration (human layer, not just tools)
- "Just ask Claude" as the core insight
- Ownership of files/data as freedom
- The weird BAT energy, not hustle-bro optimization
- Screen recordings of actual workflows
- One-on-one guidance through the scary parts

---

## What Doesn't Land

- "Build your own tools" (too generic, too hustle-bro)
- Pure productivity framing ("save 10 hours a week")
- Feature lists of what Claude Code can do
- Technical tutorials without the mindset layer
